Folder 'utils' - These are usually generic, reusable functions that can be used throughout the project. They often do not have any dependencies on the rest of the application. Highly reusable across different parts of the application. Often stateless and pure functions, meaning they don't depend on or alter the state of the application.

Folder 'helpers' - More specific data, typically used for helper functions or modules that assist with specific tasks or components within the application. These functions might be more closely tied to the business logic of the application or specific to certain components or features. Often more specific to certain parts of the application. Might depend on the application's state or specific components. Can sometimes be a bit more complex and tailored to the application's needs.
